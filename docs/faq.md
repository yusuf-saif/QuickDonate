# FAQ

## Does QuickDonate expose my secret key?

No. The secret key is only used server-side during transaction verification.

## When is the thank-you email sent?

Only after a donation is verified successfully on the server.

## Are preset and custom amounts both supported?

Yes. Preset amounts are configurable, and custom amounts can be enabled or disabled.

## Does the plugin track amount type?

Yes. The donation log stores whether the donor used a preset amount or a custom amount.

## Can I add more gateways later?

The architecture is ready for more gateways, but only Paystack is implemented right now.
